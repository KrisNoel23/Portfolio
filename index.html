About Me

I build things
that matter.

My path into tech wasn't traditional — and that's exactly what I bring to the table. Growing up in Brooklyn in a Trinidadian immigrant household, I had no roadmap into software engineering. I found my way through The Marcy Lab School, driven by curiosity and the realization that the technology I grew up with was built by people just like me.

I'm a Software Engineering Fellow who loves the full picture — frontend feel, backend structure, and everything that connects the two. I care deeply about building with empathy and intention, especially when it comes to products that serve real people.

Outside the code editor, I'm lifting weights, traveling, writing, or reading something that shifts my perspective. I believe the best engineers never stop being students.
